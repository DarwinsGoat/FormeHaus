How It Works

A simple, artist-friendly process - from file to finished piece.

1. Send Your Print-Ready 3D Model

Upload your STL, OBJ, or other print-ready file. Models must be complete and ready to print - no editing or sculpting services included.

2. Approve Scale, Material & Pricing

You'll receive a clear quote with recommendations, sizing, materials, and timeline.

3. Print, Finish & Deliver

Your piece is printed at high resolution, optionally surface-finished, inspected for accuracy, and shipped safely to your studio. Brooklyn-area pickup available.

About FormeHaus

Artist-led fabrication rooted in precision, craft, and creative practice.

FormeHaus was founded in Brooklyn, NY by fine artists with over a decade of experience working across photography, sculpture, and digital fabrication. With a deep understanding of form, material, and the demands of artistic production, FormeHaus bridges the gap between traditional craft and modern technology.

Every print is produced with the same attention to detail and technical rigor that defines fine art practice - precise layer resolution, careful material selection, and an understanding of how prints will be used in casting, mold-making, and exhibition contexts.

Unlike commercial fabrication services, FormeHaus approaches each project with an artist's eye. You're not working with a machine shop - you're collaborating with someone who understands sculptural form, surface quality, and the creative process. High-fidelity prints.
